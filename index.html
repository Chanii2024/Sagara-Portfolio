A classical guitarist turned visual virtuoso, Sagara Lakmal De Mel brings a musician's rhythm to the art of photography.

Honored with the Nelson Mandela Peace Award (2019) and recognized as a Global Achiever, he has served as the Chief Official Photographer for the President of Sri Lanka. From the Sydney Opera House to the Vatican's Papal Visit, his work transcends borders.
